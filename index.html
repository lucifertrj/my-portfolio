About Me

Tarun Jain is a Data Scientist at AI Planet and is recognized as a Google Developer Expert in AI/ML. He enjoys contributing to open-source projects and maintains two repositories: OpenAGI, for building AI Agents, and BeyondLLM, for quick experimenting, evaluating, and observing RAG pipelines. He was also part of Google Summer of Code 2024 at Red Hen Lab.

Over the past two years, he has been a part of DeepLearning.AI's Pie & AI Ambassador program, where he curates content for his YouTube channel, AI with Tarun. Furthermore, Tarun has given over 30+ talks including 5 international in-person talks as a speaker.

If you're interested in collaborating on a project or discussing a coding challenge, feel free to reach out to me.

My Tech Stack: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Transformers, Generative AI, Large Language Models, Langchain, LlamaIndex
